Lua Forge
wiki

LuaForge (http://luaforge.net/) is a free service to the Lua community that hosts and catalogs many Lua projects.

As of October 2009, there are plans to replace LuaForge with something new, with less emphasis on the hosting (e.g. what GitHub, Google Code, and SourceForge.net do) and more emphasis on the cataloging. Discussions on this are being held in [1] and design documents are at [2]. A related project under way is a code snippets side similar to but more organized than SampleCode.
