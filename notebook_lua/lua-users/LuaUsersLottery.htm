Lua Users Lottery
wiki

The Lua User's Lottery is a way to determine who will pay for costs associated with lua-users.org.

The winner is selected randomly by a computer script via a public seed from http://random.org/.

If the lottery is open, you may register by adding your name and contact info (email address or link to page containing such) to the list below. A wiki name alone is sufficient if your page contains contact info. Examples:

  • JohnDoe?
  • John Doe, <john@example.com>
  • John Doe, http://lua-users-lottery-fan.example/~jdoe/

WARNING: Make no payment unless instructed by a securely signed email from the lottery organizer.

Contributions are not tax-deductable.
